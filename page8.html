Twine Project

Title: PRESS START!

Dimension: 1920X1080 screen resolution

Medium: TWINE Software Program

Brief Artist Statement: Being a gamer has led me to the field of game development which I want to pursue but I also know a good amount of history about the world of video gaming. My knowledge of the history of video gaming ranges from games of the 1980s to game developers of the 1980s. I want to test the knowledge of gamers and non-gamers alike by using the Twine program. I have utilized this program by making it into a multiple choice questionnaire for the audience.
