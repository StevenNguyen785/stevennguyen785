Brief Artist Statement: I am employed as part-time for a career coaching company called "Super Purposes," where I have the title as a (DVS) or Digital Video Specialist. My duties for the company is currently working as a video editior using Adobe Creative Suite Apps such as Adobe Premiere Pro. This is a 100% remote work based company and I'm collabrating with artistic professionals for example, Graphic Designers and Comedy Content Writers. There are two different series I'm currently working on for Super Purposes where one episodic series is called "Career Changers." This series is about how to help individuals convert from on different field of work to another in 12-Weeks. The second series I am working on is also episodic, is a Docuseries that is based on a "12-Week Career Coaching Program" on how to get the job "you want" in that amount of time. These two projects are work-in-progresses.
