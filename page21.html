Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: Adobe Premiere Pro editing with Adobe Photoshop using the quick selection tool.

Project Summary: In 2022, I completed a remote internship with Super Purposes, a career coaching company, where I served as a Digital Video Specialist (DVS). My primary responsibilities included video editing using Adobe Creative Suite applications such as Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects. Throughout the internship, I collaborated with a diverse creative team, including graphic designers and comedy content writers. During my time with the company, I worked on two episodic series: "12-Week Career Coaching Program" – A docuseries designed to guide individuals toward securing their ideal job within a 12-week timeframe. This project has been completed, and I earned an IMDb credit for my contributions. "Career Changers" – An ongoing series focused on helping individuals transition into new career fields, also within 12 weeks. I edited several episodes, including Week 5, Episodes 2 and 3, which are currently in post-production. Although my internship has concluded, the work I completed is being retained for the final cut and continued by another intern.
