Net Art

Title: Choose Your Destiny

Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: HTML & CSS and Photoshop

Brief Artist Statement: As an artist, I love to add morals or life lessons within my artworks. With this net art project, I have created a "choose your destiny" scenario through the use of html and css. I want the audience or in this case the player to click on different links of how they want to go about life from either choosing being lazy or not working hard at all in life. Once the audience or the player chooses the right path, they will be rewarded at end.
