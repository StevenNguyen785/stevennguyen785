Title: Gym Life Shenanigans Game

Dimension: 800x600

Medium: p5.js coding

Brief Artist Statement: I have enjoyed playing video games my entire life and developing one out of the series I have created which is Gym Life Shenanigans is an honor. I have used Javascript or p5.js source code to develop this game. In order to the play the game, the player controls the sole character of "Former Buff Guy." The player uses the space bar to jump to avoid the incoming dumbells coming from the right. if the player gets hit by the dumbells than the player loses and the game pauses. In order to play again, just refresh the page.
