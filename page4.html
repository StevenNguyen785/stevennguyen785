3D Print Project

Title: Ying Yang Rings

Dimensions: 2.5x2cm & 2x1.5cm

Medium: 3D printer with PLA material

Brief Artist Statement: I brought my Ying-Yang Minecraft digital 3D art to physical life by the use of 3D printing. I turned it into artistic rings because it is a unique type of artwork that a person can wear similar to tribal masks which are considered by many artist around the world as “artistic masks.” I created 2 different size rings so I can make it as my very own personal art collection or set.
