QLA - Prologue

CONTINUATION OF THIS STORY IS COMING SOON IN A 2ND VIDEO...

Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, Fakeyou.com and different clips from YouTube.

Brief Artist Statement:

This purpose of this project is about storytelling by downloading different clips and audio using them to

create the story I want to convey. I have downloaded

stock video, Youtube videos, licensed & royality free audio from a YouTube video and music extension for the Opera GX browser.The narration of the video is by me, Steven Nguyen.

The outro of the video I have completely created from Adobe After Effects and everything else I used Adobe Premiere Pro.The main reason why I want to work on this 3 part video

project is because its a prologue to 2 other videos I will be uploading soon. The target audience I want to make this video for are for sci-fi horror fans ages 13-45 but I want

to keep this content PG-13 in audience rating as much as possible. I want to demonstrate to clients and companies alike that are watching this project, that I have the skillset to

put different clips together and develop a understanding storytelling video which a lot of employers are seeking.
