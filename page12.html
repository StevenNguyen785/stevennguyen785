P5 Randomizer Project

Title: Zombie Randomizer

Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: HTML&CSS with P5 sketch

Brief Artist Statement: The zombie culture which I like to refer it as, has been going on since George A. Romero's hit 1968 film “Night of the Living Dead.” As a teen, I have always been fascinated with the zombie genre and I want to demonstrate that with this project. This zombie culture in today's society has spread in popularity like a zombie virus in the 21st century with critically acclaimed films and tv shows such as "28 Days Later", "The Walking Dead TV show", and "REC". There are also video games that are based on the zombie genre as well for example "Resident Evil", "House of the Dead", and "Dead Island". I have downloaded pics from all these zombie games and movies from the internet, then I assembled them to create a p5 Javascript function feature called the “Randomizer.”
