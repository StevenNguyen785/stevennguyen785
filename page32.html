60 sec. Demonstration of Hollywood News Reel/Stories

Medium: Adobe Premiere Pro, Raw and Edited footage from Santa Cruz Spine & Sport.

My Project Summary: This project highlights my skills in video editing by presenting examples of how I transformed raw footage into engaging social media reels.

Reel One: - Chiropractic Demonstration I worked part-time and on a freelance basis with Santa Cruz Spine & Sport, a chiropractic practice. My responsibilities as a Marketer included filming content, editing video, and designing custom thumbnails—incorporating both video production and graphic design elements to support the company’s branding and outreach.

Reel Two:Hollywood Gossip – Brad Pitt and His Children This reel explores the media narrative surrounding Brad Pitt and his children. I curated and compiled a variety of footage featuring Brad Pitt both on-screen and off-screen to construct a cohesive and engaging story. The final piece includes a custom voiceover to guide the viewer through the visual content and enhance the storytelling.
