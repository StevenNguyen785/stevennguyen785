Maya 3D Animation

Title: New and Nostalgic

Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: Maya 3D animation software

Brief Artist Statement: This artwork was inspired by a very popular artistic YouTube channel called “Cool 3D World.” The channel is about the use of 3D animation but the style of it in particular is in a way adult oriented and also somewhat weird and crazy which I am very intrigued with. I have created my very own type of animation with Cool 3D World's idea and made it kid friendly. The reason I titled my piece “New and Nostalgic" is because this artwork is a mix of the classic stop-motion and next generation digital animation. The sole character was supposed to be a Sasquatch. However, during the development process, I decided to make it into a unique looking character instead.
