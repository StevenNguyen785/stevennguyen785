MineCraft Art

Title: Ying Yang in Minecraft

Dimension: 1100 × 688

Medium: Adobe Photoshop and Minecraft Video Game

Brief Artist Statement: In the popular video game Minecraft, I created or crafted a digital Ying-Yang symbol sculpture which is considered to be by many artists as digital 3D art. I usually but not always incorporate life lessons and morals to my artworks. The moral of the Ying-Yang itself is that "An unbalanced universe will bring chaos to the universe but once you combine light and dark or negative and positive energies together it will create a balance that will ultimately create peace."
