VR Version

Title: DON'T PRESS THE FIRE BUTTON?

Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: Adobe Premiere Pro editing with actual costumes and props.

Brief Artist Statement: There has been a stigma or stereotype when it comes to playing violent video games because of how it negatively influences a youth or even adult's minds by them imitating what they are seeing on the gaming screen. The popular video game franchise "Grand Theft Auto" has been blamed by activists and political leaders alike because of how this gaming series is part of the main cause of violent acts throughout the world. I wanted the audience or the viewer to watch this documentary project and decide for themselves if violent video games cause violent tendencies towards gamers or non-gamers alike. I have also made a VR version of this documentary project.
