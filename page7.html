Title: Purgatorium Game

Dimension: 1920x1080

Medium: Twine Software, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Adobe Photoshop

Brief Artist Statement: The Telltale video game company has developed non-linear storyline games where it gives the player the choice of how the storyline of the game ends. I have developed a similiar Telltale style game but with a horror movie twist where the player chooses the "destiny" of the sole protagonist, Marty. In order to play the game, it just utlitizes the mouse to point and click.
