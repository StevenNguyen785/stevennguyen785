Artist Statement

Welcome to My Official Artist Portfolio Website!

This website showcases my collection of digital media arts, from digital imagery to web development. The software applications that I use range from Adobe Photoshop to Audacity. With these digital artworks, I will show a digital online art exhibition that will give the audience or viewer a combined emotional feeling of color, humor, exhilaration, and most of all entertainment. You will explore a variety of mediums, from different sounds to Virtual Reality. During the development stages of working on these projects, I came to an epiphany of what my interest and future goals would be. My main interests are computer programming and video production. Besides reaching my dream goal to become a professional digital media artist, I want to motivate and help other artists and non-artists alike. Famous digital media artist that have inspired me to pursue my dream are Takeshi Murata, Nam Jun Paik, and Mary Ellen Bute. If someone were to ask me what my “ultimate” goal of becoming an artist would be, my answer would be to become a movie director. Well-known film directors like James Wan, Justin Lin, and Christopher Nolan have inspired me to pursue video editing and film production. My overall message or main theme that I want to give the audience or the viewers through my artworks are morals and life lessons. Positvity will basically be the easter egg in most of my pieces.

I hope you enjoy exploring my digital artworks and THANKS FOR VISITING MY PORTFOLIO WEBSITE!
