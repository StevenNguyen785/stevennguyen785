About Me

WELCOME TO MY ONLINE PORTFOLIO: Here you will find a collection of my video art and creative projects, ranging from documentary-style storytelling to animation. These works have been developed using tools from the Adobe Creative Suite, including Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Photoshop. In addition to my creative software skills, I am also proficient in Microsoft Office applications such as Word and Excel. I maintain a personal YouTube channel showcasing a progression of my work—from college projects to professional and current endeavors. My hands-on experience includes working in studio environments, live stage setups, and outdoor shoots, where I’ve operated cameras, lighting systems, audio equipment, and green screens. Outside of my creative work, I enjoy video gaming and fitness. When asked what drives me as an artist and why I enjoy working with clients and companies, my answer is simple: I have a deep passion for storytelling. To me, video editing is a powerful tool for conveying narratives—it's the story that comes first, with characters and visuals following to bring it to life. I value working in collaborative environments where artists, managers, and clients contribute to a shared vision. While solo creators may work independently for personal gain, I find fulfillment in being part of a team where success is shared and mutual growth is encouraged. Lastly, I strongly believe in the importance of constructive critique. Honest feedback is essential for growth and improvement, and it helps me continue to evolve as a professional artist..
