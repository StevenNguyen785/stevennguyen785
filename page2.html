Meme Art

Title: Steven's Blind Date Experience

Dimension: 1100 × 786

Medium: Adobe Photoshop

Brief Artist Statement: When it comes to internet memes, it's basically about story telling in today's pop culture. I used Adobe Photoshop to develop a meme based on one of my bad experiences of blind dating. I find memes to be similar to mail art, which is where a person mails an artwork in any medium to a random individual and that individual will make their own artwork out of it and then pass it along to someone else. Hopefully someone else will add some sort of storytelling to my blind dating meme shoud it ever go viral.
