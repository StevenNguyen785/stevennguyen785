Title: Soar Kidz Book Debate Club Intro Video

Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: Adobe Premiere Pro and Zoom Recording Video

Project Summary: SOAR KIDZ is a online privately held advanced classes

for students K-8th grade. The "Soar Kidz Book Debate Club Intro Video" is a introduction video for parents who have an interest for their child to learn more about online student debating. The original video is from Zoom recording from an actual teacher who works for SOAR KIDZ along with one of the co-founders of the company which I have edited out from co-founders request.
